Digital Health literacy

Digital health literacy is important especially for vulnerable social group.
The research said the lower level of education and income, the lower digital literacy.
Digital health literacy depends on personal empowerment, professional expertise, incecntive, etc.
There are a lot of program in Korea to empower digital literacy.
We pay attention to the elder people's digital literacy.
The reason importance of digitital literacy especially for elder people is related to the ability to maintain health. It is directly related to public health equity.
